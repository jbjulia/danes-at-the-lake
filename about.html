About

My name is Barbara Reed and I am the owner of Danes at the Lake located in North Mississippi. For many years, I often dreamt of sharing my life with a Great Dane after falling in love with the breed when a babysitter allowed me time to bond with her male Merle. Time passed and that dream eventually came to fruition 2 years ago when Jeff McVay, owner of Deep South Danes in Abbeville, Mississippi gifted us with Scarlett, who has since become our focus and such a major factor in our lives. My new dream now is to spread her love to other deserving individuals and families.
